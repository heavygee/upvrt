1. Information We Collect

When using UpVRT, we collect:

  • Your Discord user ID and username

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Authenticate you with Discord
  • Verify your server membership

3. Data Storage

Video files are temporarily stored during processing and are immediately deleted after upload or if an error occurs. We do not permanently store any uploaded content.

We use Discord's services for authentication and file sharing. Please refer to Discord's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.
